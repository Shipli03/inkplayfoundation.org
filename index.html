About us

A thinking partner for complex change

InkPlay Foundation helps organisations navigate complex development and sustainability challenges through strategy, evidence, learning and stakeholder engagement.

We work with governments, development agencies, foundations, private sector organisations and civil society institutions to strengthen systems, improve decision-making and achieve meaningful outcomes.

By combining analytical thinking, technical expertise and practical experience, we help organisations translate ambition into action and evidence into impact.
